# Build Burger Sim!

## Repository
<https://github.com/OllieCarson/final_project.git>

## Description
Build Burger is about the struggle of trying to find work in this day and age as an artist,
and how often we have to find jobs that are not what we want to do. Build Burger has you making burgers
for customers while you scrounge up your savings to afford to focus on art.

## Features
- Each ingredient will go back and fourth at the stop of the screen. when you press space, the ingredient will fall on the burger
	- each ingredient is drawn and appended onto the scene. it will go across the X axis at a decided speed. upon key dress down, it will go down along the Y axis.
- theres a randomly generated order ticket with the order on it.
	- order ticket is appended onto the scene with random ingredients, the ingredient instantly appear as you drop them on the burger, There is no choosing the right ingredient. 
- when each burger is made, money is given and put into a "art fund" jar.
	- art fund jar recieves amount of money based on accuracy of the burger

## Challenges
- I need to learn how to randomize the appending of images and how to swap them out.
- I need to learn how to make an image go back and fourth the X axis.
- I need to learn how to determine the accuracy of the burger, and how to connect that to the amount of money recived.

## Outcomes
Ideal Outcome:
- An order ticket is randomly generated, the first part of the burger is the same as the order tickets first part, it goes back and fourth across the X axis, upon space being pressed, it drops the ingredient on top of the previous ingredient, after the burger is built, it determines how close it is to the "Perfect" burger, and money is put into the "Art Fund" based on that score.

Minimal Viable Outcome:
- The ingredient goes across the screen, upon space being pressed it falls onto the previous ingredient.
